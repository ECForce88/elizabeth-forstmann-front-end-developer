About Me.



I am a former teacher, new to the software development scene. The more I learn, the more I want to learn! I have always been passionate about technology in the classroom from game-based learning to innovative collaboration and presentation software. I first learned to code with blocks alongside my students, and I was hooked. I left the elementary classroom to teach middle school computer science courses, challenging myself to stay a few steps ahead of my students and develop new curriculum. While I loved sharing my passion with my students, I decided to pursue a career in software development and web design. I'm thrilled to use my skills and eye for design to bring your project to life from concept to code!
